Our lab, in partnership with Improbable Beef, is pushing the boundaries of the synthetic meat market. While other labs have had success createing meat alternative from algae, fungi, and other microoganisms, we believe even more sustainable sources can be found. Our goal is to discover the ideal bacteria species to make synthetic beef and early research has shown the perfect candidate may be very close to home: in our own belly-buttons!
